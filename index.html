ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

ABOUT

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple hand game played between two people, but here, you'll play
against the computer.
Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with their hand:

  1. Rock (✊): A closed fist
  2. Paper (✋): An open hand
  3. Scissors (✌️): A fist with the index and middle fingers extended

RULES

Open the console (press F12) to play and compete with the computer.
There's a total of five rounds.

  • Rock beats Scissors (Rock crushes Scissors)
  • Scissors beats Paper (Scissors cut Paper)
  • Paper beats Rock (Paper covers Rock)
  • If both players choose the same shape, it’s a tie
